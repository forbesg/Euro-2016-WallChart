About the Euro 2016 Wallchart

The Euro 2016 Wallchart allows you to update the scores of the tournament as it unfolds

On first load, data is loaded into your browsers local storage. This means that you can complete the wallchart and every time you open it in the browser again your data will have been saved.

From the fixtures page you can find all the fixtures from the current day, plus the previous and next days fixtures.

On the Groups page you will see all the group tables for the group stages. As you complete the scores on the fixtures page the tables will update all the information for you.

Click on the Group table title to take you to a view of that particular Groups table and fixtures or access from the Groups dropdown menu in the main navigation.
